Welcome!

This wiki is for anyone interested in the imaginary world of Alahnvaer, created by Paul Highum.


Currently it contains a map on the home page as well as pages for many of the known races of the world. Each race page contains a description of the race as well as the geographical range of the race (the regions and cities in which the race commonly lives)


There is also a page for adding a race to the world, temporarily, to allow a user to test their imagination within the score of Alahnvaer.


I hope you enjoy this introduction the Alahnvaer and if you have any questions about it feel free to contact me
